

  Job Description      This position is responsible for:          Reviewing contractor change order requests and routing them through the Project Management team for validation and approval.          Generating purchase requisitions for new and/or additions to existing purchase orders.          Conducting first review of contractor invoices ensuring proper backup and verification of formulas and total actuals to date are accurate.          Tracking budget value of each contract against the current approved committed contract and notify the Project Management team when funds are running low.             Requirements:        Bachelor"s degree in construction management, engineering, or related field from accredited program. 3-6 years of experience in contract administration.          Good understanding of contract types, change management, and invoice reviews.          Past experience with purchasing platforms is a plus.           Strong analytical and problem solving skills.          Advanced MS Excel skills.          Excellent written & verbal communication skills.
